How to Play:

  • Under the black box is a picture.
  • Guess which one is it from those visible pictures and click it
  • For each wrong guess, your chance reduce (10 to 0)
  • If your guess is correct, your correct number increase (from 0 to 3)
  • Once your chance hit 0, you'll lose the game and if your correct guess hit 3, you'll win.
  • The show button helps you uncover the black box and see the picture under it, but you can only use it once in the game.

Good luck!
